Cardiology

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that deals with the disorders of the heart as well as some parts of the circulatory system. Cardiologists diagnose and treat conditions such as heart disease, heart attacks, and heart failure.

Cardiologists

Neurology

Neurology is a medical specialty dealing with disorders of the nervous system. Neurologists diagnose and treat conditions such as epilepsy, Alzheimer's disease, stroke, and multiple sclerosis.

Neurologists

Gynecology

Gynecology is the branch of medicine that deals with the health of the female reproductive system. Gynecologists diagnose and treat conditions such as ovarian cysts, cervical dysplasia, and infertility.

Gynecologists

Rheumatology

Rheumatology is a specialty of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to joints, muscles, and connective tissues. Rheumatologists treat conditions such as arthritis, lupus, and fibromyalgia.

Rheumatologists

Endocrinology

Endocrinology is the branch of medicine that deals with the endocrine system, including hormones and glands. Endocrinologists diagnose and treat conditions such as diabetes, thyroid disorders, and hormonal imbalances.

Endocrinologists

Dermatology

Dermatology is the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders. Dermatologists treat conditions such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer.

Dermatologists

Gastroenterology

Gastroenterology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the digestive system and its disorders. Gastroenterologists diagnose and treat conditions such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), Crohn's disease, and gastric ulcers.

Gastroenterologists

Oncology

Oncology is the branch of medicine that deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer. Oncologists treat various types of cancer using treatments such as chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgery.

Oncologists

Pulmonology

Pulmonology, also known as pneumology, is the branch of medicine that focuses on the respiratory system.This medical specialty deals with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and conditions affecting the lungs and respiratory tract.

Pulmanologists

Psychology

Psychology is the scientific study of the mind and behavior. It encompasses a wide range of topics, including how individuals think, feel, and behave, as well as the underlying processes that influence these thoughts, emotions, and actions.

Psychiatrists
